# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue in MIKLIUM, please report it responsibly. You can reach us through:

* Email: miklium@outlook.com
* In a private message on Discord to _owners_ of MIKLIUM ([they can be found on our server](https://discord.gg/6HUJTXh45t))
* By creating private [secutiry advisory](https://github.com/MIKLIUM-Team/MIKLIUM/security/advisories/new)

Include in your report:

* What the problem is
* Steps to reproduce it
* Any potential impact or risks

**Please do not make the issue public until we’ve addressed it.**

## Our Response

Once a report is received:

1.  We’ll acknowledge it within 48 hours.
2.	Investigate and verify the issue.
3.	Fix it as quickly as possible.
4.	Notify you when it’s resolved.

## Recognition & Thanks

Thank you for helping us make MIKLIUM safer for everyone! As a token of our gratitude, we will publicly thank you and display your username in our release notes and in a special thanks section for helping with the development. Your contributions make a real difference!
